VEGETABLE SALAD

Benefits of Vegetable Salad

Salad greens contain Vitamin A, Vitamin C, beta-carotene, calcium, folate, fiber, and phytonutrients

Steps to make to Vegetable Salad

  • Choose Your Base. Red & Green Leaf Lettuce. Sweet, delicate flavor.
  • Add Fruits & Veggies. Avocado. Cucumbers.
  • Mix in Crunch. Toasted Nuts. Carrots.
  • Amp Up the Flavor. Cheese. Scallions.
  • Drizzle & Done! Delicato Extra Virgin Olive Oil
Leafy vegetables are a good choice for a healthful diet because they do not contain cholesterol and are naturally low in calories and sodium.
